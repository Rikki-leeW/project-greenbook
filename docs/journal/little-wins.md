#The first heartbeat
12-7-2026
I've gone from thinking ChatGPT would create an app for me in 15 minutes, to spending two days downloading software, setting up tools I'd never heard of, and copying and pasting code to create the beginning of an app... that somehow became an entire world.

Tonight, I finally clicked a button, filled in a form, and got to type:

"It worked."

It felt like it had a heartbeat.

It was 7:00 pm on Sunday, 12 July 2026.

Sprig, the Garden Helper, might become a thing, or it might not.

But for now, it's giving me a little purpose, and I'm learning new things along the way.

It's not helping me grow seeds and plants.

It's helping me grow too.
